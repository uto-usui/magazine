## Software

As I hinted at earlier, my software requirements are _incredibly_ lean. To do my job the only applications I really ever need are Terminal and Chrome. Of course, Terminal is a broad way of covering a lot more software, so I‚Äôll outline that stuff below.

Chrome is my browser of choice, and I use it for everything internetty (except browser testing, naturally).

1.  [More Colour Formats?](#more-colour-formats)

If, like me, you frequently need the official[1](#fn:1) Core Web Vitals colours for slides and presentations, here you go:

-   **Good** `#09ce6b`
-   **Needs Improvement** `#ffa400`
-   **Poor** `#ff4e42`

## More Colour Formats?

**Good**

-   RGB: `rgb(9, 206, 107)`
-   HSL: `hsl(150, 92%, 42%)`
-   HWB: `hwb(150, 4%, 19%)`

**Needs Improvement**

-   RGB: `rgb(255, 164, 0)`
-   HSL: `hsl(39, 100%, 50%)`
-   HWB: `hwb(39, 0%, 0%)`

**Poor**

-   RGB: `rgb(255, 78, 66)`
-   HSL: `hsl(4, 100%, 63%)`
-   HWB: `hwb(4, 26%, 0%)`
